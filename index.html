Imagine a world where brave knights, known as the Comma Knights, are on a mission to save their kingdom from the menacing Grammar Goblins. These goblins have been stealing punctuation marks, creating chaos in the land of Sentenceville. Write an exciting story (300-500 words) about the Comma Knights as they embark on their quest. Be sure to include encounters with various mythical creatures and challenges along the way.


Incorporate the following elements into your story:

  • The knights must confront the Grammar Goblins and retrieve the stolen punctuation.
  • Each knight possesses unique powers based on different punctuation marks (e.g., Sir Period, Lady Exclamation).
  • The knights face a critical decision: whether to fight or negotiate with the goblins.

Your story must include at least:

  • Five complex sentences.
  • Five compound sentences.

Use all four comma rules covered in class:

  • Place a comma before coordinating conjunctions connecting two independent clauses.
  • Do not place a comma when a single subject performs two actions.
  • Place a comma after an introductory dependent clause.
  • Do not use a comma when the dependent clause follows the independent clause.
